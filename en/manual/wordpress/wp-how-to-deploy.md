# Zip WordPress files

Zip all WordPressfiles, in **ZIP** format, excluding the following


    1. .git
    1. .idea
    1. .gitignore
    1. .htaccess
    1. error.log
    1. wp-content/backup-xxx 
    1. wp-content/wp-super-cache/
